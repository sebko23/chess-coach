Thank you for your interest in **CHESS COACH** (the "Project"), maintained by **[PROJECT ENTITY]** (the "Project Owner"). In order to clarify the intellectual property license granted with Contributions from any corporate entity, the Project Owner must have a Corporate Contributor License Agreement ("CCLA") on file that has been signed by an authorized representative of the Corporation.

This version of the Agreement allows a Corporation (the "Corporation") to submit Contributions to the Project Owner, to authorize Contributions submitted by its designated employees to the Project Owner, and to grant copyright and patent licenses thereto.

If You have not already done so, please complete and sign, then scan and email a PDF file of this Agreement to **[PROJECT EMAIL — TO BE SET]**, or submit it via the CLA-bot procedure described in §11 below.

## 1. Definitions

**"You"** (or **"Your"**) shall mean the copyright owner or legal entity authorized by the copyright owner that is making this Agreement with the Project Owner. For legal entities, the entity making a Contribution and all other entities that control, are controlled by, or are under common control with that entity are considered to be a single Contributor.

**"Contribution"** has the meaning set out in `CLA-ICLA.md` §1, applied here to submissions by the Corporation or its Designated Employees.

**"Designated Employees"** means those employees of the Corporation whose names appear on Schedule A attached hereto, as amended from time to time by the Corporation by providing written notice to the Project Owner.
